Efemérides (Component)

Efemerides is an easy component for Joomla to manage important historical events.

A good complement to this Component: Efemérides (Module)

Support Joomla 1.5, 1.6 and now Joomla! 2.5.

Important: The versions 0.9b or greater of the extension are for Joomla! 2.5.
